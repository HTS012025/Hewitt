Hewitt Compliance Consultancy provides independent support to organisations seeking FSC® and PEFC™ Chain of Custody certification.

With over 10 years of practical industry experience and an NVQ Level 4 qualification in Wood Science and Technology, we help businesses achieve and maintain certification.

We also provide timber product specification support and stress grading services in accordance with EN 14081:2005+A1:2011 and BS 4978:2007.
